About the Event

Join us for a comprehensive FRCS Course in Plastic Surgery. This one-day event will be held at the prestigious Royal Free Hospital in Hampstead, offering participants an unparalleled learning experience.

The Royal Free FRCS Plast course has been running annually for many years, boasting a high pass rate for delegates attending. The course includes lectures and viva-based scenarios with consultants and successful candidates, providing tailored feedback, hints, and tips about passing the exam.

Faculty Profiles

Mr. Dariush Nikkhah

Mr. Nikkhah underwent his specialist training at several prestigious units including the world-renowned Queen Victoria Hospital, Great Ormond Street Hospital, Royal Free Hospital, and The Royal London Hospital. He also had further training in Australia at The Royal Perth Hospital in the field of Microvascular Surgery. This has enabled him to be skilled in a broad range of Cosmetic and Reconstructive procedures. Mr. Nikkhah is an international authority on Microvascular surgery. He is regularly invited to international meetings and he has published over 130 articles in plastic surgery and 2 textbooks.

Mr. Yazan Al-Ajam

Mr. Ajam is a plastic surgeon with a subspecialty interest in hand surgery. He completed his training in the pan-Thames rotation and undertook a specialist hand fellowship in Manchester.
